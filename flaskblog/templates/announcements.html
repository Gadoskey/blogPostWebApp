  • Replies: Allow users to reply to comments, creating threaded discussions for more in-depth conversations.
  • Sharing: Implement sharing options so users can share posts on social media or with others.
  • Post Categories/Tags: Add categories or tags to posts to help users easily find content relevant to their interests.
